About Us

Minions Early Learning Daycare is dedicated to nurturing children's curiosity and preparing them for kindergarten through a play-based approach with Montessori basics.

The daycare manager, Shima Javanmardi, is an experienced Early Childhood Educator (ECE) graduate who has been trained with the latest scientific teaching methods in Canada. She has extensive experience in child development and learning techniques, including Montessori, play-based learning, and music-based education.

At Minions Early Learning Daycare, we focus on fostering children's cognitive, social, and emotional growth to prepare them for school. Our programs are designed to enhance critical thinking, problem-solving skills, creativity, and independence.

Programs & Fees

Our daycare programs include:

  • Infants (0 to 18 months) - Focused on nurturing, sensory exploration, and early development.
  • Toddlers (18 to 36 months) - Encouraging independence, social skills, and cognitive growth.
  • Pre-school (3 to 5 years) - Structured learning with play-based methods to prepare for kindergarten.
  • Kindergarten Before & After School Care - Supportive environment for early school years.
  • Grade 1 to Age 12 Before & After School Care - Homework assistance, creative activities, and social engagement.
